Meet the team

Katie Rayden-Drabkin

Founding Partner

Katie brings 23 years of experience of getting stuff done to Group Ava. Having served as the executive and personal assistant to world-class Hedge Fund CEOs, Early-Stage Founders, Portfolio Managers, and the Chairman of the New York Philharmonic Orchestra, Katie is well adept at juggling multiple projects and has been described as the "indispensable swiss-army knife" of executive and personal support. Katie's outgoing personality and 'anything is possible' attitude makes her a delight to work with, keeps her calm under pressure, and powers results. Katie is British and after 30 years in NYC, now splits her time between the US, UK and Europe.

Anne-Marie Hayden

Founding Partner

Anne-Marie emigrated from Ireland to New York City in 2005 and holds dual citizenship. She currently lives in Spain and works as a Front-End Web Developer. While in NYC, she gained a BA in Economics and 10 years of experience as an Executive Assistant. As an EA, supporting C-suite executives in both philanthropy and finance, Anne-Marie was considered a vital resource and highly regarded for her tenacity and creativity with problem solving, critical thinking and team spirit.

Leslie Hutchings

Founding Partner

Leslie brings 15+ years of professional experience in non-profit management, strategy development, Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion (DE&I), and providing business support and guidance to entrepreneurs and founders. While she wears many hats in her day-to-day, Leslie is best known for her proven ability to establish structure and develop plans that lead even the earliest of ideas to life. She has led DE&I initiatives, created designed business education for founders, developed content for virtual and in-person events, built communications strategies, and led the development of research and strategic plans. Everything started with an idea which she nurtured into reality. An enthusiastic collaborator, Leslie adds positivity, flexibility, and a strong work ethic to every project. With a BA in Anthropology and an MS in Strategic Communications from Columbia University, Leslie excels in a people-first approach, and listens to the unique needs of her clients through every step of their partnership. Leslie lives in NYC, and has traveled the world extensively. When she is not providing business support, she is likely planning her next trip and loves to offer recommendations when asked.
